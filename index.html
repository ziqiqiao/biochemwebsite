Our Core Solutions

BioChem has two core products that, together or individually, provide real-time optimization of the aeration function or secondary biological process unit.

BACS

BioChem has developed an advanced aeration control system to effectively control the residual dissolved oxygen (DO) concentration in each aerobic zone of an activated sludge wastewater treatment process.

Learn More

BIOS

The Bioprocess Intelligent Optimization System (BIOS) is an integrated hardware and software system that enables real-time monitoring and control of wastewater treatment operations based on the dynamically changing biological activity occurring in the bioreactor.
